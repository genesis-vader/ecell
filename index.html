Initiatives

E-Entrepreneurship cell was launched and aims at working for the following initiatives for the benefits of the budding startups and entrepreneurs.

Alumnus Entrepreneurship

An initiative by e-cell rgpv to facilitate the startups of the institutions and have interactive session with the entrepreneurs.

e - Mentoring Program

If you are an aspiring startup with an idea and need support on it, ECell RGPV is the platform for executing it. Regiser with us and we will assist you in making your dream come true. We'll give you the platform to question our mentors.

Startup Internship Program

EStartup internship program (SIP) strives for developing an entrepreneurial streak among campus students by helping them acquire an internship in most spirited and exciting startups running around. For the startups , we will provide a platform to enlist the best students from the institution.

Campus Outreach Program

Establishing a networking with other e cells by having campus representative in other campus to promote entrepreneurship system across colleges and student entrepreneurs.

Rural Entrepreneurship

Promoting the entrepreneurial ideas and unrevealed and potential ideas from various sections of societies among the entrepreneurial network and get the best out for them in events .
